Services

Fruitful Results

These are medicines that are rarely found in most pharmacies. You only need to upload their image among the rare medicines to reach the largest base of pharmacies. If they are not found in pharmacies, OKSGEN will search and provide your medicine through its partnership with pharmaceutical companies located in Saudi Arabia or agents of foreign companies, and after providing it, OKSGEN will Communicating with you, just needs a little time.

Medicine donation service

If you want to donate your medicines, you are indispensable or in excess of your needs and would like to help others, just click on the donate icon inside the application and take pictures of the medicine so that the expiry date is clear in the pictures and write the amount that you intend to donate and then write Her prescription and finally click on a donation and may God reward you a thousand good.

Free medication service

These are the free medicines that have been donated by customers who are in excess of their needs, benefactors, pharmacies owners, pharmaceutical companies, or charitable and civil organizations, so that each customer in need of these medicines can photograph his medical prescription through the free medicine icon inside the application.

Prescription Services

Where you can get your medicine through your prescription by uploading a picture of the prescription from your studio or by photographing it with the application's camera and completing your order.

Online Pharmacy Store

It is a store where all your medical supplies and cosmetics are included in the price all you have to do is add them to your cart and complete your order.
